About Us

At ProPunts we are passionate about sports and friendship. ProPunts has been developed to help every sports fanatic live out their dreams through Fantasy Sports. We decided we would help the fantasy sports community by building a platform that allows not just fantasy football or fantasy basketball, we provide access to all variations of fantasy sports on one simple platform. With an array of leagues and sports ProPunts has grown to become one of the leaders in the fantasy sports space.
