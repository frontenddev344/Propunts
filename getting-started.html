Playback Guide

The ProPunts Fantasy Sports app couldnt be any easier to navigate, but were always here to help! So lets take a look at how to begin your fantasy sports career. This guide will take you step by step through the full process of setting up your ProPunts account and your fantasy sports squad!

Getting Started

Install ProPunts

  • On your device open the app store
  • Download the ProPunts Fantasy sports app
  • Install the app
1

Create a ProPunts account

  • Use social media account or your email to sign up
  • Create a safe and secure password
  • Verify yourself with government identification
2

Complete the app tutorial

  • After logging in the tutorial pop up will show
  • Learn how to use the app
  • Check out different sections like news and squads
3

Create a ProPunts squad

  • Use our auto draft tool or handpick your squad
  • Make informed decisions with our player stats
  • Dont go over budget youve got $100 Million be careful
4

Find a league

  • Join an open league or create a private one for yourself and your mates!
  • Take part in cup competitions throughout the season
5

Tips for selecting your squad

  • Keep up to date with form, injuries and suspensions
  • Dont blow your budget on 1-2 players its a team game!
  • Use our player stats to make informed decisions
6

Manging your squad

  • Keep up to date with injuries and suspensions to make weekly adjustments/transfers
  • Keep an eye on your players form
7

Make transfers

  • Make transfers but be careful or market changes, the player you sell might cost more to re-sign
  • Strtegically make transfers, take into account the injury/suspension length
8

Get to grips with the scoring system

  • Learn what gains points and loses them
  • The best players to eye might not get you the most points
  • Consistent 5 point players are better than players that win 10 one week and 2 the next
9

Get the win

  • Compete in our leagues and put yourself to the test
  • See if you really could make it as a top level manager
10

Enjoy the process

  • Experience the buzz of fantasy sports management
  • Interact with the community in our forums
  • Talk tactics, hidden gems and just all things sports
11

Thank you for joining ProPunts fantasy sports app. Propunts goal is to get sports enthusiasts in touch with each other, form friendships, compete and just have fun. We hope you enjoy your journey and make friends and memories along the way. From the team at ProPunts Fantasy sports best of luck!
